// PROJECT_03
Smart Safety Wearable for Industrial Workers
Built a multi-parameter IoT monitoring wearable for coal miners on ATMEGA328p, tracking 5 critical safety parameters simultaneously: temperature, humidity, LPG & methane concentration, and blood pressure. Integrated DHT11, MQ-6, MQ-4, and pulse sensors with 20×4 LCD display. Implemented SPI protocol for inter-component data exchange and GSM + GPS modules for automated hazardous-condition alerts with live location transmission to supervisors.
